March 3, 1953

the morning was clear.
waves crashed steadily into the rocks.
checked the lamp and the lenses, everything was in order.
some gulls circled overhead for some time, then moved on.
they amaze me, their freedom.
kept the log, inspected the keep, cleaned the rails a bit.
no ships today, nothing unusual.
the light holds steady, as it always does.
